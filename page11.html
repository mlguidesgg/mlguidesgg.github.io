ALL UNITED

Minsitthar inspires the whole team. When teammates slay enemy heroes, he obtain extra 60 Gold wherever he is; whenever Minsitthar himself slay enemies, all his teammates obtain extra 15 Gold

1ST SKILL

SPEAR OF GLORY

Minsitthar stabs ahead with the Spear of Glory, dealing 175(+180% Extra Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage to all enemies along the way. When pulling back the spear, he will also pull towards himself the first enemy the spears hit, dealing 150(+80% Extra Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage and knocks the target back.

2ND SKILL

SHIELD ASSAULT

Minsitthar awakens the strenght of the Shield and releases the ppower within forward. The power exploded upon hitting the first enemies unit and deals 239(+160% Extra Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage to all enemies in the area. Meanwhile, he obtains 200(+100% Total Physical ATK) Shield and increases 20% Movement Speed immediately. The first enemy will be stunned 1s and the other enemies will be knocked back. 

ULTIMATE

kING'S CALLING

Minsitthar charges a short distance forward and calls 4 Royal Guards to form a field and fight together with him. Each attack of Royal Guards deals (+70% Total Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage. The Royal Guards cannot move, but they'll attack enemies in the field first. When staying in the area, Minsitthar increases 30 points of Physical and Magic Defence. Enemies in the field are slowed by 20% and cannot use directional Blink skills.
