AKAI

TANK
INITIATOR / CROWD CONTROL

PASSIVE

TAI CHI

After using a skill, Akai will gain a shield that absorb up to 6% of his max HP. Lasts 2s. The effect activates every 2.5s.

1st skILL

THOUSAND POUNDER

Akai jumps towards a designated location. Upon landing, he deals 300(+50% total Physical ATK) points of Physican Damage to enemy units, and slows them by 30% for 2s. When Akai deals damage to enemies marked with Blender, they will get stunned for extra 1s.

2ND SKILL

BLENDER

Akai fires a frog-shaped wave in the designated direction. The wave explodes upon hitting enemies or once it reaches its Max range, dealing 350(+120% extra physical ATK) points of physical damage to enemies and marking them for 5s.

ULTIMATE

HURRICANE DANCE

Akai spins like tornado for 3.5s and knocks back the surrounding enemies, dealing 180(+100% extra physical attack) points of Physical Damage (Damage decays againts the same target).
Using this skill removes all Debuffs from Akai immedietly. He also becomes immune to slowing effect in the duration and increse 30 point of his movement speed.
