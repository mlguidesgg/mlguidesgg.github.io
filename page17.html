KEY INSIGHT

Harith gains insight from his Key. He immediately reduces its tim controlled up to 45% (dependent on the number of nearby enemy heroes).

1ST SKILL

SYNCHRO FISSION

Harith creates a phantom of himself in the opposite direction and releases Synchro Fission, dealing 120(+70% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to all enemy units on the path. When the 2 Hariths fuse together, an explosion occurs dealing 360(+210% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to all enemies in the explosion area.

2ND SKILL

CHRONO DASH

Harith dashes towards the designates direction. When arrives, he steals Magic Attack of nearby enemy heroes, generates shield that absirbs atleast 150(+150% Total Magic Power) points of damage and enhance the next Basic Attack. Harith's enhance Basic Attack deals 200(+80% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage and slows enemies by 40%. Hitting an enemy with Harith's enhance Baic Attack deal reduces the CD of the next dash 3s. 

ULTIMATE

ZAMAN FORCE

Harith uses his key to Zaman. When the multidimensional rifts appears, the CD of Chrono Dash is reduced by 4s.
