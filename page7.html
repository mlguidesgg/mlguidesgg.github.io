INITIATOR / CROWD CONTROL

pASSIVE

SPELL CURSE

Khufra activates the Spell Curse left by Esmeralda every 12s to increase the range of the next basic attack, dealing Maging Defence equal to (+120% Total Physical Attack) plus 6% of his Max HP to and slowing them by 30%. Lasts 1.5s. Meanwhile, Khufra restores 8% of his Max HP. Each time Khufra uses his skills to control any enemy heroes, The CD of Spell Curse will be reduced by 4s.

1ST SKILL

TYRANT'S REVENGE

Khufra pulls the bandage on his arms to launch himself in the specified direction, dealing Physical Damage equal to 80 plus 10(+1.5% Extra Physical Attack)% of his max HP to all enemy units on the path. When blinking to the furthest distance or encountering a first enemy hero, Khufra will immediately stop, dealing Physical Damage equal to 80 plus 10(+1.5% Extra Physical Attack)% of his Max HP to enemies nearby then knocking them in the air for up to 1.1s.

2ND SKILL

BOUNCING BALL

Khufra uses bandage to wrap himself into a magic bouncing ball, increasing his own Physical & Magic Defence by 75%. Enemies trying to use blink skills to move across Khufra will be knocked airborne. Each time the magic bouncing ball hits the ground, it will deal Magic Damage equal to 30 plus 6(+2% Extra Physical Attack)% of his max HP to enemies nearby and slow them by 80%. Last 0.5s.

ULTIMATE

TYRANT'S RAGE

Khufra pulls back all enemies target around him towards his front, dealing 300(+100% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage andslowing them down for 1.25s. if the enemies are knocked againts the wall, extra Physical Damage equal to 150% of this skill's damage will be inflicted upon them. and they will be stunned, instead of slowed.
