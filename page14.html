pASSIVE

DREAMLAND TWIST

Lunox has lost all her sense of time and realty. As a result, she does not benefit from CD reduction effects. When Darkening becomes more severe, CD effects become Magic PEN. When Brilliance becomes stronger, CD effects become Physical and Magic Defence.

1ST SKILL

STARLIGHT PULSE

Lunox summons a rain of Starlight upon nearby enemies and deals 200(+110% Total Magic Power) point of Magic Defence, recovering 60(+20% Total Magic Power) points of HP. The recovering effect double when the skill hits enemy hero. Gains one stack of Power of Order after use.

2ND SKILL

CHOAS ASSAULT

Lunox unleaches chaos energy forward, dealing 240(+120% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage and slows enemies by 10%. Lasts 1s. When attacking heroes, Lunox deals extra Magic Damage equal to 2.0% points of enemy hero's HP.
Gains one stack of Power of Order after use. 

3RD SKILL

COSMIC FISSION

When Lunox uses the power of Order and Chaos, she will deal 300(+150% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Defence to the targets along the way and slowig them by 40%. Lasts 2s.                                                 

ULTIMATE

ORDER & CHAOS

Lunox is twisted by the powers of Choas and Order. Every time she uses her powers, they become stronger.
When she uses the power of Order, she gains Brilliance and deals continuous damage in an invulnerable state.
When she uses the power of Choas, she gains Darkening and can blink in a designated direction, canceling the CD of Chaos Assault.
