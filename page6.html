LOLITA

TANK / SUPPORT
PUSH / CROWD CONTROL

PASSIVE

NOUMENON ENERGY CORE

if Lolita takes no damage in 5s, Noumenon Energy Caore will provide shield that can take 300(+40*Hero Level) points of damage, lasting 20s. Lolita's nearby allies can get shields while approaching her.

1ST SKILL

CHARGE

Lolita dashes in the designated direction. In next 4s, her next Basic Attack will take her to the enemy target,dealing Physical Damage equil to 200(+100% Total Physical Attack) plus 7.5% of targets Max HP. Meanwhile, the target will be stunned for 0.8s. Deals 200(+100% Total Physical Attack) plus 50% of target's Max HP to minions.

2ND SKILL

GUARDIAN'S BULWARK

Lolita raises her shield to block all incoming Ranged Basic Attacks and Projectiles.
Use again: Lolita launches Energy Blast, dealing 400(+50% Total Physical Attack) to 880(+110% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage. The more Projectiles and Basic Attacks the shield blocks, the more damage Energy Blast will deal.

ULTIMATE

NOUMENON BLAST

Lolita gains a shield from her Passive. Then after charging for 2s, she deals 350(+100% Total Physical Attack) to 700(+100% Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage in a fan-shaped area  in front of her. When casting at Max Charge, Lolita will also stun her enemies for 2s(tap again to cast immediatly). When charging, Lolita slows enemies in tha fan-shaped area by 75%.
