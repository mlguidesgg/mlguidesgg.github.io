FIRAGA ARMOR

X-Borg's Firaga Armor inherits of his max HP and takes all damage for x-borg while it's active. When the Armor runs out of HP. X-Borg wil do a roll to the direction of the Left Joystick to disengage from the armor.

1ST SKILL

FIRE MISSILE

X-Borg activates the flamethrower and fires in the designated direction, dealing 40 (+50% Total Physical ATK) points of Physical Damage for 2s. Enemies whose temperature rises to the limit will take True Damage and slow down by 20%
Armorless State: X-Borg adjusted the flamethrower to narrow angle and lengthen the distance, reducing the damage to 60% of that in armor state.

2ND SKILL

FIRE STAKE

X-Borg shots 6 Fire Stakes at the edge of the fan-shaped indicator. 1.7s later, the stakes are taken back into his body. Each stake deals 50 (+20% Total Physical ATK) Physical Damage to enemies in the path and pulls them back.
Armorless State: X-Borg adjust the Fire Stake Launcher to send the stake futher away and shortens the gap between the stakes.

ULTIMATE

LAST INSANITY

X-Borg charges forward in a designated direction, shooting fire around dealing 200 (+100% Extra Physical ATK) and slowing enemies by 25%.
Use again: End the flame spray and self-explode right away
